## Project Overview
GreenRoots Community Garden is a non-profit organization founded in 2015, aimed at promoting urban agriculture and environmental awareness.  
The website will showcase the organization's mission, events, volunteer opportunities, and donation options.

---

## Website Goals and Objectives
- Increase online visibility and attract new volunteers and donors.
- Promote events and workshops.
- Provide educational resources on sustainable living.
- Improve communication with the community.

---

## Key Features and Functionality
- **Homepage**: Highlights mission and vision.
- **About Us Page**: Information about the organization.
- **Events Page**: Calendar of upcoming workshops and activities.
- **Volunteer/Donate Page**: Forms for volunteer registration and donations.
- **Contact Page**: Contact form and embedded map.
